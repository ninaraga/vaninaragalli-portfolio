UX/UI Design

UX, User Experience design focuses on how well the product works and how the user feels when using it, whereas UI, User Interfaces design focuses on how that software product looks. Both are equally important and play a vital role when creating and developing software. A pretty UI may attract visitors, but if the product is not functional, the visitors won't stick around. And if the product works properly but doesn't look good, users will feel frustrated and look elsewhere to satisfy their needs. There needs to be a balance.
